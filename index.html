About

The Crypto Cobra Club (CCC) is a collection of 10,000 Cobra profile picture NFT's that slither through the Ethereum Blockchain. Holders who own 2 cobras will be airdropped a snakelet, which grants access to our rogue-like game. Holders will also have access to our one time claim of our VENOM token and our burn token, which will be used to breed more snakelets.

Why Join the Club?

Game Development DAO

Voting rights for production of the game/direction of our video game, involvement in the DAO, which includes the naming of the game.

IDO Whitelist for VENOM

Access to pre-sale for the VENOM IDO, which powers the entire ecosystem. VENOM allows the user to breed snakelets.

Airdrops for Holders

Holders will receive multiple NFT or Token airdrops, including a one-time claim of VENOM Tokens depending on how many CCC NFTs they own

Snakelet Airdrop

Holders will be airdropped a Snakelet per 2 CCC NFTs they own, this will be the only way to acquire a snakelet.

FAQ

When Will Crypto Cobra Club launch?

The launch date is to be determined. TBD

How Many Crypto Cobras are There?

There will be 10,000 cobras in the collection. The 1/1s are included in the mint.

Where can I mint a Crypto Cobra NFT & How can I Obtain it?

You can mint a Crypto Cobra Club NFT off our minting website that we will connect to our main website.

How Much Does it Cost to Mint a Crypto Cobra?

The cost of minting one Crypto Cobra is 0.08 ETH + gas fees.

What Does $VENOM Do?

$VENOM will be required to breed snakelets. Breeding snakelets is important because it allows more players to play the game and enter the ecosystem.

How Do I Get Access to the Game?

Snakelets will be required to play the game and will determine your starting characters' stats and perks for the Rogue-like game.

What is a Snakelet & How Can I Acquire One?

Snakelets will first enter the ecosystem by an airdrop to holders owning 2 Crypto Cobra Club NFT's.
